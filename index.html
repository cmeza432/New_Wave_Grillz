How does it work?

The process is pretty simple


Once we agree on the product you want to order
we then send out a molding kit with detailed instructions
on how to create your mold


When we receive the mold, it would then take anywhere
from 7-10 business days to finish the final product


Down payment is needed for us to ship out mold to get started!!!
The amount may vary but usually has to be half of total price!


Once finished, we reach out to you with pictures of the
product to verify finished piece is as requested


It would then take anywhere from 3-5 business days to recieve
your brand new Grill!
